THE VISION

One Beam of Light.
Seven Decisions.

In architecture, engineering, and construction — data is light. Raw, white, overwhelming. It enters the project as drawings, specs, schedules, budgets. Without a prism, it scatters — lost in silos, buried in emails, evaporated at handover.


AEC Prism is that prism. We take the chaos of building data and refract it into clear, actionable spectrum: planning, design, construction, handover, operations, intelligence.

Six phases. Fifty-one applications. One platform.
